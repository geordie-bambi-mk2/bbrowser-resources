What Changed In v6.3

Video time limit reliability and bug fixes.

Fix

Time Limit Now Works

Video time limit was silently ignored when the browser hadn't loaded video metadata yet. It now always fires.

Skip

True Auto-Skip via VLC

With Skip videos that exceed limit selected, VLC is polled for the real duration after launch and stopped within seconds if too long.

Reliability

Fallback Hard Stop

If VLC never reports a duration, a hard stop fires exactly at the configured time limit as a last resort.
